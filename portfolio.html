Portfolio

A few of my projects during my time at Fullstack Academy:

First, this portfolio itself was the very first project we had to do. It was a little rough but after my 6 month class, I came back to edit it and make it the material you're looking at today!

Here are some different projects, in order of when they were due. Just click the name of the project and it will take you there!

1. Guess the number- The computer picks a random number and will give you a hint and 5 guesses.
2. Connect 4- Play the family game-night classic against a friend or against the computer!
3. The Puppy Bowl- Add dogs to or remove dogs from the roster. Click on "see details" to learn more about the dogs you see on screen!
4. The Art Collector- Learn about various paintings, sculptures, and artifacts in the Harvard Art Museums' private collection!
5. Strangers' Things- Like Facebook Marketplace, use Strangers' Things to buy or sell any item you can find!

Unfortunately, the projects following this list were all done through Heroku, who stopped their free service in November of 2022. With that, I was only allowed to have 5 files on their site without being forced to pay for it, meaning I had to delete the links to some projects to make way for new ones towards the end of my class. However, I am in the process of working on different side projects. Feel free to ask me about them!
